Kili - AI powered podcast tool for language learners

Python, PyTorch, NLTK, Go, AWS, Terraform, NextJS

kili.tarkalabs.com - ‚ñ∂Ô∏è - Kili is a language learning tool that uses an ensemble of models to help you learn new human languages. I wanted to build an AI powered solution that does not use the OpenAI APIs.

I consumed RSS/ATOM feeds to download podcast episodes. I then transcribe the text with whisper. While whisper can do translations, I found that the quality of translations was quite poor. So I decided to use another model. I also found that whisper does not tokenize sentences properly. So, I reconstructed the sentences from the transcript using a our custom sentence tokenizer using NLTK. I then generated sentence embeddings with SBert and indexed them with QDrant. I used a model called flan-t5 to generate translations to English, Spanish and German. I also found that translating directly from French to German did not lead to good results but using English as a bridge language yielded better translations.

Tunecore - Platform Architecture (Distribution)

Software Architecture, Platform engineering, SRE, Ruby on Rails, Go, PostgreSQL, AWS services

Tunecore helps artists sell their music worldwide. It takes albums and singles from artists all around the world and distributes them to over 50 music stores like Apple, Spotify, Youtube Music, Amazon Music, etc. We then process the sales reports and process payouts. It paid out over $1 billion to artists for the year 2021.

I helped them split up a 10-year Rails monolith based on Rails 3 into different services. We built the core distribution service into its own service on Rails 6. We also split the transcoding piece and moved it to AWS lambda. We evaluated AWS elastic transcoder and AWS elemental media file convert. We instead used Lambda with a statically compiled FFmpeg layer. This approach roughly had a 5x-6x throughput increase over the elastic transcoder. We also moved the actual SFTP delivery of the files to Go and refactored the job queue to three independent steps. This resulted in over 70% reduction in the infrastructure costs for the production cluster.
